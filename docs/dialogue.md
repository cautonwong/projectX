**Planning a Weekend Get-Together**

**Characters:**
*   **Sarah:** (Organizing a small party)
*   **Tom:** (Helping Sarah with the planning)

**Dialogue:**

**Sarah:** Hey Tom! Thanks for coming. We **need** to finalize the party details.
**Tom:** No problem, Sarah. I **am** happy to help. What **is** on the agenda?

**Sarah:** First, the guest list. Everyone **is** invited, of course, but some of my friends **are** still unsure.
**Tom:** That's fine. Each of your cousins **has** confirmed, right?
**Sarah:** Yes, that's correct. And the news **is** that my brother, along with his two kids, **is** also coming.
**Tom:** Great! The whole family **is** going to be there then.

**Sarah:** Almost. Neither John nor Mary **has** replied yet. I hope they can make it.
**Tom:** Hmm. Either they **are** busy, or they **don't** check their messages often.
**Sarah:** Probably the latter. Anyway, the main dish **is** pasta.
**Tom:** Sounds delicious! And what about drinks? A lot of options **are** usually available.
**Sarah:** This time, a variety of juices **is** what I'm planning. Also, some of the sparkling water **is** already in the fridge.

**Tom:** Perfect. Now, about the music. My band **plays** mostly rock, but we can do some pop too.
**Sarah:** That's awesome! The band members **are** all so talented.
**Tom:** Thanks! Many a musician **dreams** of playing at such a fun gathering.
**Sarah:** You're too kind! There **are** a few things left to do, like setting up the decorations.
**Tom:** And here **is** the box of balloons you asked for.
**Sarah:** Oh, thanks! Mathematics **is** not my strong suit, but I think we have enough for the whole room.
**Tom:** I'm sure we do. Everything **looks** ready!

---

**主谓一致原则示例讲解：**

1.  **Sarah:** Hey Tom! Thanks for coming. We **need** to finalize the party details.
    *   **主谓一致点:** `We` (复数主语) + `need` (动词原形，复数谓语)。
    *   **原则:** 复数主语使用复数谓语。

2.  **Tom:** No problem, Sarah. I **am** happy to help. What **is** on the agenda?
    *   **主谓一致点:** `I` (第一人称单数主语) + `am` (动词be的对应形式)。`What` (单数疑问代词) + `is` (单数谓语)。
    *   **原则:** `I` 搭配 `am`。疑问句中，谓语动词与实际主语（agenda）保持一致。

3.  **Sarah:** First, the guest list. Everyone **is** invited, of course, but some of my friends **are** still unsure.
    *   **主谓一致点:** `Everyone` (不定代词，单数) + `is` (单数谓语)。`some of my friends` (部分主语，friends是复数) + `are` (复数谓语)。
    *   **原则:** `everyone`, `everybody`, `everything` 等不定代词作主语时，谓语动词用单数。`some of` 后接可数名词复数时，谓语动词用复数。

4.  **Tom:** That's fine. Each of your cousins **has** confirmed, right?
    *   **主谓一致点:** `Each of your cousins` (主语是 `Each`，单数) + `has` (单数谓语)。
    *   **原则:** `each of`, `every one of` 后接复数名词时，谓语动词用单数。

5.  **Sarah:** Yes, that's correct. And the news **is** that my brother, along with his two kids, **is** also coming.
    *   **主谓一致点:** `the news` (形式为复数，意义为单数) + `is` (单数谓语)。`my brother, along with his two kids` (主语是 `my brother`，`along with` 引导的短语不影响主语单复数) + `is` (单数谓语)。
    **原则:** 某些名词（如 `news`, `politics`, `mathematics`）形式为复数但意义为单数时，谓语动词用单数。当主语后跟 `as well as`, `along with`, `together with`, `besides`, `except`, `but`, `like`, `including` 等短语时，谓语动词与前面的主语保持一致。

6.  **Tom:** Great! The whole family **is** going to be there then.
    *   **主谓一致点:** `The whole family` (集体名词，作为一个整体) + `is` (单数谓语)。
    *   **原则:** 集体名词（如 `family`, `team`, `class`）被视为一个整体时，谓语动词用单数。

7.  **Sarah:** Almost. Neither John nor Mary **has** replied yet. I hope they can make it.
    *   **主谓一致点:** `Neither John nor Mary` (由 `neither...nor` 连接的并列主语，谓语与靠近的 `Mary` 保持一致) + `has` (单数谓语)。
    *   **原则:** 当主语由 `either...or`, `neither...nor`, `not only...but also` 连接时，谓语动词与靠近的那个主语保持一致。

8.  **Tom:** Hmm. Either they **are** busy, or they **don't** check their messages often.
    *   **主谓一致点:** `Either they...or they...` (由 `either...or` 连接的并列主语，谓语与靠近的 `they` 保持一致) + `are` (复数谓语) / `don't` (复数谓语)。
    *   **原则:** 同上，谓语动词与靠近的那个主语保持一致。

9.  **Sarah:** Probably the latter. Anyway, the main dish **is** pasta.
    *   **主谓一致点:** `the main dish` (单数主语) + `is` (单数谓语)。
    *   **原则:** 单数主语使用单数谓语。

10. **Tom:** Sounds delicious! And what about drinks? A lot of options **are** usually available.
    *   **主谓一致点:** `A lot of options` (由 `a lot of` 修饰的复数名词 `options`) + `are` (复数谓语)。
    *   **原则:** `a lot of`, `lots of`, `plenty of`, `most of`, `some of` 等短语修饰可数名词复数时，谓语动词用复数。

11. **Sarah:** This time, a variety of juices **is** what I'm planning. Also, some of the sparkling water **is** already in the fridge.
    *   **主谓一致点:** `a variety of juices` (主语是 `a variety`，单数) + `is` (单数谓语)。`some of the sparkling water` (部分主语，`sparkling water` 是不可数名词) + `is` (单数谓语)。
    *   **原则:** `a variety of` 后接复数名词时，谓语动词用单数。`some of` 后接不可数名词时，谓语动词用单数。

12. **Tom:** Perfect. Now, about the music. My band **plays** mostly rock, but we can do some pop too.
    *   **主谓一致点:** `My band` (集体名词，作为一个整体) + `plays` (单数谓语)。
    *   **原则:** 集体名词被视为一个整体时，谓语动词用单数。

13. **Sarah:** That's awesome! The band members **are** all so talented.
    *   **主谓一致点:** `The band members` (复数主语) + `are` (复数谓语)。
    *   **原则:** 复数主语使用复数谓语。

14. **Tom:** Thanks! Many a musician **dreams** of playing at such a fun gathering.
    *   **主谓一致点:** `Many a musician` (形式上是 `many a` + 单数名词，意义上是“许多”，但语法上是单数) + `dreams` (单数谓语)。
    *   **原则:** `many a` 后接单数名词作主语时，谓语动词用单数。

15. **Sarah:** You're too kind! There **are** a few things left to do, like setting up the decorations.
    *   **主谓一致点:** `There are a few things` (倒装句，谓语 `are` 与后面的实际主语 `a few things` 保持一致) + `are` (复数谓语)。
    *   **原则:** `There be` 句型中，`be` 动词的单复数与后面真正的主语保持一致。

16. **Tom:** And here **is** the box of balloons you asked for.
    *   **主谓一致点:** `here is the box` (倒装句，谓语 `is` 与后面的实际主语 `the box` 保持一致) + `is` (单数谓语)。
    *   **原则:** `Here be` 句型中，`be` 动词的单复数与后面真正的主语保持一致。

17. **Sarah:** Oh, thanks! Mathematics **is** not my strong suit, but I think we have enough for the whole room.
    *   **主谓一致点:** `Mathematics` (形式为复数，意义为单数) + `is` (单数谓语)。
    *   **原则:** 某些学科名称（如 `mathematics`, `physics`, `economics`）形式为复数但意义为单数时，谓语动词用单数。

18. **Tom:** I'm sure we do. Everything **looks** ready!
    *   **主谓一致点:** `Everything` (不定代词，单数) + `looks` (单数谓语)。
    *   **原则:** `everything`, `everyone`, `everybody` 等不定代词作主语时，谓语动词用单数。
